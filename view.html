About World

The world is a vast and diverse planet comprising seven continents, each with its unique characteristics and cultures. These continents are Asia, Africa, North America, South America, Antarctica, Europe, and Australia. Asia is the most populous continent and contains the highest number of countries, with 49 nations in the region. Africa follows closely, with 54 recognized countries. Europe consists of 44 countries, while North America has 23 countries, and South America has 12 countries. Australia, often referred to as Oceania in a broader context, includes 14 countries. Antarctica, although not inhabited permanently by civilians and not sovereignly divided, is recognized as a continent.
